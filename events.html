The Symposium

On November 16th, the Network will host a one-day symposium at Royal Central School of Speech and Drama.


The symposium will include:


  1. reports back from network activities at the two Lab events and the structured online exchange that has continued between them
  2. presentations from researchers from outside the core Network group
  3. workshops
  4. performances
  5. an interdisciplinary keynote speaker.


Artists, performers, critics, producers, and academics are all welcome. However, numbers will be limited, and initially by invitation. Enquiries about attendance can be sent to PIINetwork@cssd.ac.uk
